# Code of Conduct: Community Standards
## 1. Our Pledge
In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment, we as contributors and maintainers pledge to making participation in our project and our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## 2. Our Standards
We are committed to providing a safe, productive, and happy environment for all participants.

## Expected Behavior
- **Be Inclusive:** Acknowledge and respect different perspectives and experiences.

- **Be Collaborative:** Focus on what is best for the community and the project’s data integrity.

- **Be Respectful:** Use welcoming and inclusive language in all critiques and pull request reviews.

- **Gracefully Accept Constructive Criticism:** View feedback as an opportunity for professional growth.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- The use of sexualized language or imagery and unwelcome sexual attention or advances.

- Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks.

- Public or private harassment of any kind.

- Publishing others' private information, such as a physical or electronic address, without explicit permission (doxing).

- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

## 3. Consequences of Unacceptable Behavior
Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying the standards of acceptable behavior and are expected to take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any instances of unacceptable behavior.

If a participant engages in behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, the project maintainers may take any action they deem appropriate, including:

- **A Private Warning:** A formal notice of the violation and a request to cease.

- **Temporary Suspension:** A short-term ban from the project repository and communication channels.

- **Permanent Expulsion:** A permanent ban from the project and community for repeated or severe violations.

## 4. Reporting and Enforcement
Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by reaching out in the Instagram groupchat.

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated and will result in a response that is deemed necessary and appropriate to the circumstances. The project team is obligated to maintain confidentiality with regard to the reporter of an incident.
